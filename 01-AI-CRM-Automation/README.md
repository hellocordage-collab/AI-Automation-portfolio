# AI CRM Lead Management Automation

## Business Problem

Businesses lose leads because customer information is managed manually.

This causes:

- Slow response times
- Missed follow-ups
- Manual data entry
- Poor customer experience

---

## Solution

This workflow automates the complete lead management process.

### Workflow

Manual Trigger

↓

Lead Information

↓

AI Analysis

↓

Lead Qualification

↓

Google Sheets CRM

↓

Automatic Email

↓

Telegram Notification

---
